Description:

The black blueberry is a plant with long crawling roots emitting multiple branched trunks. It requires fresh climates to have a correct development. It bears rounded berries of 5mm of diameter of a dark blue, almost black colour. The common blueberry or European black blueberry belongs to the family of Ericaceous, and its scientific name is Vaccinium myrtillus.
